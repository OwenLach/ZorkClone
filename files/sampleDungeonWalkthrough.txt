Sample Dungeon Walkthrough
--------------------------

Upon spawning into Impractical Westeros, you should first pick up the dagger by
typing "take dagger" in order to defend yourself. Then, type "n" to move north
to The Wall where you'll be attacked. Typing "stab dagger" 3 times will finish
off the attacking Murr. Typing "s" 6 times will leave you at King's Landing
where Q hangs out at. Type "take necklace" and then type "wear
necklace" to wear the necklace and increase your score by 1. Go "e" 2 times,
and then type "heat GreenEgg" to hatch a baby green dragon. Type "feed GreenDragon" to
increase your score again by 1. Type "take BlackEgg" to take the black dragon
egg. Go "w" two times and then type "trade" to enter the trade menu with Q. Type
"dagger,necklace,BlackEgg" to offer your inventory which is worth 40 credits. 
Then type "soul" for the Soul of the Lords valued at the same 40 credits, and you should recieve the item. 
Lastly, you can optionally examine it by typing "examine soul". Type "absorb soul" to consume the lord's soul, completing the game.
